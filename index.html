Hello, I'm Ryoko

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, find me in the forest, riding down my pistachio colored bike on slopes, or keeping my hands busy in one of the urban gardens.

I was born and raised in Japan, and arrived to Queens NYC in 2005. The following year we moved to Washington D.C. I love the expansive tree canopy of the city and diverse cultural scenes fitting soundly in one place.

My favorites: Starting projects from scratch, arranging family albums, gluten free baking on Sundays, indulging in artisanal ice cream on the rooftop, traveling and staying at Airbnb, sketching people’s profiles… to name a few.

A fun fact: I can sit down on a chair half way, with my back straight for a pretty long time thanks to the muscle memory cultivated when I played clarinet in the middle school band.
